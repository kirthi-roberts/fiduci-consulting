About

Built on experience.

Fiduci Consulting was founded in 2014 by Kirthi Roberts, PhD, P.Eng. — a licensed professional engineer with over 25 years of experience across multiple industries.

What began as an engineering recruitment practice has grown into a firm that serves organizations and professionals across three interconnected disciplines: finding exceptional talent, supporting workforce transitions, and coaching technical leaders to operate at the highest level.

Based in Vancouver, BC, Fiduci works with clients across Canada and the United States.

The Fiduci difference: We are the only firm that combines deep technical recruitment expertise with AI-powered career intelligence and an active placement network. When we help your people transition out, we also help them land.

Careers are built on decisions, not just qualifications.

The right role at the right time changes everything. Whether you are hiring or being hired, Fiduci ensures both sides of the equation are aligned for long-term success.
